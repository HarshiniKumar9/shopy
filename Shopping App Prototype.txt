Shopping App Prototype

Description:

This is a prototype of a shopping app designed to enhance online shopping experiences. Developed using Figma, the app features a user-friendly interface and intuitive navigation.

Features:

Product Browsing: Users can easily browse through a variety of products.

Shopping Cart: Allows users to add products and view their selections.

Wireframes & Mockups: Includes initial wireframes and high-fidelity mockups to visualize the app design.

Development Tool Used: Figma
